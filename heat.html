Water has a high heat capacity

The amount of heat a substance must absorb to increase 1 gram of the substance by 1℃

WHY?

Hydrogen bonds cause water to have high heat capacity.

B.A.R.F.

  • In order to break a hydrogen bond, heat must be abosorbed. (B.A.)
  • In order to form a hydrogen bond, heat must be released.(R.F.)
