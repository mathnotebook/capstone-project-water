Water is polar

WHY?

A water molecule contains two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom.

The unequal sharing of the electrons will cause the water molecule to have an asymmetrical shape and be polar.Â 

  • oxygen's electronegativity=4.4
  • hydrogen's electronegativity=2.2
  • This results in the oxygen having more force to pull the hydrogen to that side, leading in an unequal sharing of electrons.

Ex: Tug of War game. If one side is stronger than the other, the rope will be inclined toward the side with more strength.

Vocabularies:

  • electronegtivity:the ability of an atom to attract electrons
  • polar:electron distribution is unbalanced
